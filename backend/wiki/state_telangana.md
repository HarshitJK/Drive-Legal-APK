# Telangana — State Traffic Laws & Fine Schedule

## Overview
Telangana follows the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019. Hyderabad Traffic Police uses CCTV surveillance and smart city cameras for enforcement.

## Telangana E-Challan System
- Hyderabad Traffic Police: **htp.gov.in**
- National portal: **echallan.parivahan.gov.in**

## Key Fine Schedule (Telangana)

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Speeding | ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 (first); ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| Helmet | ₹1,000 |
| Drunk Driving | ₹10,000 (first); ₹15,000 (repeat) |
| No Insurance | ₹2,000 (first); ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| No Licence | ₹5,000 |
| No PUC | ₹10,000 |
| Seatbelt | ₹1,000 |
| Mobile Phone | ₹5,000 |
| Red Light | ₹1,000 – ₹5,000 |
| Wrong Side | ₹5,000 |

## Special Rules in Telangana
- **Hyderabad Outer Ring Road (ORR)**: Speed limit 100 km/h (cars). Speed cameras every 10 km.
- **HITEC City zone**: Special traffic management during peak IT hours.
- **Pedestrian violations**: Jaywalking fined ₹100 – ₹500 in Hyderabad.
- **No-Horn zones**: Hospitals, courts, schools. Fine: ₹500.
