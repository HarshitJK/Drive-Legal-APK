# Drunk Driving — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 185 — Driving by a drunken person or by a person under the influence of drugs.

## What is this violation
Driving after drinking alcohol or while under the influence of drugs.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹10,000 | ₹15,000 |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Drunk and Drive is listed as non-compoundable; the source table shows the fine as 0 — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
None specified in source.
