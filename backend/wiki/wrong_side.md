# Wrong Side Driving — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 184 — Driving dangerously.

## What is this violation
Driving against the authorised flow of traffic is listed as driving dangerously.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹1,000–₹5,000 | ₹10,000 |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Wrong side driving is listed as 0 and non-compoundable in the source table — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
Imprisonment for a term which may extend to one year but shall not be less than six months is stated in section 184.
