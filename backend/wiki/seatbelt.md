# Seatbelt Violation — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 194B — Use of safety belts and the seating of children.

## What is this violation
Driving without a safety belt or carrying passengers who are not wearing seat belts.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹1,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Without seatbelt: ₹1,000 — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

None specified in source.
