# Overspeeding — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 183 — Driving at excessive speed, etc.

## What is this violation
Driving a motor vehicle faster than the speed limit fixed under section 112.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Light motor vehicle | ₹1,000–₹2,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |
| Medium goods vehicle / medium passenger vehicle / heavy goods vehicle / heavy passenger vehicle | ₹2,000–₹4,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Two/three wheeler and tractor: ₹1,500 first offence / ₹2,000 repeat; light motor vehicle: ₹2,000 first offence / ₹3,000 repeat; other vehicles: ₹4,000 first offence / driving licence disqualification for 6 months on repeat — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
For a second or subsequent offence, the driving licence shall be impounded as per section 206(4).
