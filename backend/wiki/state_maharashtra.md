# Maharashtra — State Traffic Laws & Fine Schedule

## Overview
Maharashtra follows the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019 with additional state-level enforcement. Mumbai Traffic Police is one of the most technologically advanced in India.

## Maharashtra E-Challan System
- Mumbai Traffic Police portal: **mahatrafficechallan.gov.in**
- National portal: **echallan.parivahan.gov.in**
- Challans via cameras, mobile units, and e-Challan app.

## Key Fine Schedule (Maharashtra Specific)

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Speeding | ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 (LMV first); ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 (HMV first) |
| Helmet | ₹1,000; DL suspension for repeat |
| Drunk Driving | ₹10,000 + possible 6-month imprisonment (first); ₹15,000 (repeat) |
| No Insurance | ₹2,000 (first); ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| No Licence | ₹5,000 |
| No PUC | ₹10,000 |
| Seatbelt | ₹1,000 |
| Mobile Phone | ₹5,000 |
| Red Light | ₹1,000 – ₹5,000 |
| Triple Riding | ₹1,000 |

## Special Rules in Maharashtra
- **Mumbai coastal roads**: 40 km/h speed limit on sea link and coastal routes.
- **Expressways**: Pune-Mumbai Expressway speed limit 100 km/h (cars), 80 km/h (trucks).
- **No-Honking zones**: Hospital and school zones. Fine: ₹1,000.
- **Parking violations**: Towing charges ₹1,000 + fine ₹500 – ₹2,000.
- **Rickshaw/taxi**: Refusing passengers can lead to permit suspension.

## Challan Payment
- Online at mahatrafficechallan.gov.in or echallan.parivahan.gov.in
- RTO offices across Maharashtra.
- Deadline: 60 days before court summons.
