# Delhi — State Traffic Laws & Fine Schedule

## Overview
Delhi follows the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019 and has one of the strictest traffic enforcement systems in India, with extensive ATCC camera coverage and dedicated traffic police.

## Delhi E-Challan System
- Delhi Traffic Police portal: **delhitrafficpolice.nic.in**
- National portal: **echallan.parivahan.gov.in**
- Challans sent via SMS and post to registered owner.

## Key Fine Schedule (Delhi Specific)

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Speeding | ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 (first); ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| Helmet | ₹1,000; DL suspension for repeat |
| Drunk Driving | ₹10,000 + immediate DL suspension (first); ₹15,000 + imprisonment (repeat) |
| No Insurance | ₹2,000 (first); ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| No Licence | ₹5,000 |
| No PUC | ₹10,000 — very strict enforcement due to pollution |
| Seatbelt | ₹1,000 |
| Mobile Phone | ₹5,000 (first); ₹10,000 (repeat) |
| Red Light | ₹1,000 – ₹5,000 |
| Wrong Side | ₹5,000 |
| Overloading | ₹2,000 per extra passenger |

## Special Rules in Delhi
- **PUC enforcement**: Extremely strict due to air quality. Vehicles without valid PUC are impounded.
- **Odd-Even Rule**: During pollution emergencies, odd/even number plate restriction may apply.
- **No-entry zones**: Many areas have time-based no-entry for heavy vehicles.
- **Emergency Vehicle Corridors**: Violating green corridor during emergency = ₹2,000 fine.
- **Night driving**: Vehicles without rear reflectors fined ₹500.

## Challan Payment
- Pay online at delhitrafficpolice.nic.in or echallan.parivahan.gov.in
- Payment via UPI, net banking, debit card.
- Court dates issued for serious violations (drunk driving, no licence).
