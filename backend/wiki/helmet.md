# Helmet Violation — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 194D — Penalty for not wearing protective headgear.

## What is this violation
Riding or driving a motorcycle without wearing a helmet.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor cycle | ₹1,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Violation of not wearing helmet: ₹500 — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
The driver shall be disqualified for holding licence for a period of three months.
