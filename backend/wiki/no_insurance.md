# No Insurance — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 196 — Driving uninsured vehicle.

## What is this violation
Driving or allowing a motor vehicle to be driven without third-party insurance.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹2,000 | ₹4,000 |

## State Variations
All states follow central MV Act 2019 rates.

## Additional Penalties
For a subsequent offence, imprisonment for a term which may extend to three months, or fine of ₹4,000, or both is stated in source.
