# No PUCC — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 177 — General provision for punishment of offences.

## What is this violation
Failing to produce a valid Pollution Under Control Certificate on demand.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹500 | ₹1,500 |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Failed to produce valid PUCC: ₹500 first offence / ₹1,500 repeat — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
None specified in source.
