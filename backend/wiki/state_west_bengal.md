# West Bengal — State Traffic Laws & Fine Schedule

## Overview
West Bengal follows the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019. Kolkata Traffic Police is one of India's oldest and has a mix of traditional and modern enforcement.

## West Bengal E-Challan System
- Kolkata Traffic Police: **kolkatatrafficpolice.gov.in**
- National portal: **echallan.parivahan.gov.in**

## Key Fine Schedule (West Bengal)

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Speeding | ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 (first); ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| Helmet | ₹1,000 |
| Drunk Driving | ₹10,000 (first); ₹15,000 (repeat) |
| No Insurance | ₹2,000 (first); ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| No Licence | ₹5,000 |
| No PUC | ₹10,000 |
| Seatbelt | ₹1,000 |
| Mobile Phone | ₹5,000 |
| Red Light | ₹1,000 – ₹5,000 |
| Wrong Side | ₹5,000 |

## Special Rules in West Bengal
- **Kolkata yellow taxis**: Specific rules on routes and night-time operations.
- **Tram routes**: Vehicles must not block tram tracks. Fine: ₹500 – ₹1,000.
- **Maidan area**: Special traffic restrictions on public event days.
- **NH 12 (Kolkata–Delhi)**: Speed enforcement with cameras.
