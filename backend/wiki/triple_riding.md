# Triple Riding — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 194C — Penalty for violation of safety measures for motor cycle drivers and pillion riders.

## What is this violation
Riding a motorcycle with more than one pillion rider.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor cycle | ₹1,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: Drive a motor cycle with triple seat (driver): ₹1,000 — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
The driver shall be disqualified for holding licence for a period of three months.
