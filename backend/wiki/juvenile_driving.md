# Juvenile Driving — Motor Vehicles Act

## Section Reference
Section 181 — Driving vehicles in contravention of section 3 or section 4.

## What is this violation
Driving a vehicle when the driver is below the legal age, or letting a minor drive.

## Fine Range (Central — MV Act 2019)
| Vehicle Type | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Motor vehicle | ₹5,000 | Not specified in source — check echallan.parivahan.gov.in |

## State Variations
- Maharashtra: "Minor Driving" is listed as court fine; "Giving Vehicle to minors for riding" is listed as court fine and the source table notes "25,000/- 2 years jail" — Source: final-mahrastra-fines-adn-legal.pdf, Offence Details Version 21.0

## Additional Penalties
The source table notes 2 years jail for giving vehicle to minors for riding.
