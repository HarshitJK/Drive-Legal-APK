# Kerala — State Traffic Laws & Fine Schedule

## Overview
Kerala follows the Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Act 2019. Kerala has strict road safety enforcement, particularly on the NH 66 (formerly NH 17) coastal highway and in urban areas.

## Kerala E-Challan System
- Kerala Motor Vehicle Department: **mvd.kerala.gov.in**
- National portal: **echallan.parivahan.gov.in**

## Key Fine Schedule (Kerala)

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Speeding | ₹1,000 – ₹2,000 (first); ₹2,000 – ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| Helmet | ₹1,000; both rider and pillion |
| Drunk Driving | ₹10,000 (first); ₹15,000 (repeat) |
| No Insurance | ₹2,000 (first); ₹4,000 (repeat) |
| No Licence | ₹5,000 |
| No PUC | ₹10,000 |
| Seatbelt | ₹1,000 |
| Mobile Phone | ₹5,000 |
| Red Light | ₹1,000 – ₹5,000 |
| Wrong Side | ₹5,000 |

## Special Rules in Kerala
- **NH 66**: Strict speed limit enforcement (80 km/h for cars). Speed cameras deployed at frequent intervals.
- **Tourist vehicles**: Special enforcement for tourist season (Oct–Mar).
- **Night driving**: Mandatory headlights usage; driving without lights fined ₹500.
- **Hill roads**: Speed limit 30–40 km/h on ghats; overloading heavily penalised.
- **School safety zones**: Strict enforcement near schools.
